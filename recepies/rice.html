Rice and Chicken

Description

Rice and chicken is an easy and tasty meal prep you can make at home.

It is high in protein and doesent have a lot of calories./p>

Perfect for active people.

Ingridients

  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • Olive oil (preferably)
  • Chicken seasoning
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken in small cubes
  2. Place the chicken in a tray and add chicken seasoning
  3. Boil rice and add a pinch of salt while its boiling
  4. Cook the chicken on medium heat for about 7min
  5. Strain rice
  6. Add both to a plate and enjoy
