Cinnamon bun

Description

Descriptive, yes. Delicious, absolutely.

Healthy? Not so much.

But sometimes you need something a little indulgent for moments in your life that require a little something special.

Ingridients

  • Milk
  • Granulated sugar
  • Quick rise yeast
  • Butter
  • Egg + egg yolk
  • Bread flour
  • Brown sugar
  • Cinnamon
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Make a dough with milk, sugar, egg, egg yolk, butter
  2. Cover the dough and let it rise for about 1hour
  3. Spread out the dough
  4. Make a mixture with brown sugar and cinnamon and cover the dough with it
  5. Roll the dough and then cut it
  6. Bake for 20-25min on 180C
